> In a hole in the ground there lived a hobbit.
Na toll nan dor ev derio hobbit.

> Not a nasty, dirty, wet hole, full of the ends of worms and an oozy smell,
Óman 'a toll 'asp, lhecui, nimp, ha pannas ven
medhir 'a lhugir ag boss 'athebui,

> nor yet a dry, bare, sandy hole, with nothing in it to sit down on or to eat.
ag óman 'a toll harch, auns, delfui, ha cel óman na ri gans
havathad ag óman 'ans maded.

> It was a hobbit-hole, and that means comfort.
Ev nath toll 'a hobbit ri, ha so anotha gweled.

> It had a perfectly round door like a porthole, painted green
Ri ev cel hall vrodas deg sui tirel 'a cair, lentas galen

> with a shiny yellow brass knob in the exact middle
ha cel cambel 'a gangor vallen canta nan coroth deg
